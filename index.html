Project Overview

End-to-End Employee Lifecycle Management in SAP HR

This page implements the core topics from the SAP Human Resources / HCM project report. It explains how SAP supports the full employee journey, from hiring and organizational assignment to payroll, appraisal, and separation.

Problem Statement

  • No central employee data repository.
  • Mismatch between org structure and employee assignments.
  • Manual payroll and compliance risks.
  • Disconnected onboarding, leave, and offboarding flows.
  • Weak integration across HR, Finance, and Controlling.

SAP HR solves this by integrating Personnel Administration, Organizational Management, Time Management, Payroll, and Personnel Development into one auditable platform.

Foundations

Key Concepts and Terminology

Infotype

The fundamental HR data unit in SAP with date-stamped historical records.

Personnel Action

A guided infotype sequence for events such as hiring, transfer, promotion, or leaving.

Organizational Unit

A department or team node that structures the enterprise hierarchy.

Position

A specific role in the organization connected to a job and cost center.

Payroll Area

A grouping that determines payroll frequency and payroll run processing.

Wage Type

An individual salary or deduction component such as basic pay, PF, or TDS.

Employee Journey

Hire-to-Retire Lifecycle

01

PA40

Hiring

Launch the hiring action, assign a personnel number, and capture mandatory onboarding infotypes.

02

PPOME

Organizational Structure

Build and maintain organizational units, positions, jobs, reporting lines, and cost center links.

03

PA30

Employee Master Data

Maintain personal, organizational, and payroll-related infotypes with full historical accuracy.

04

PA61 / PT60

Time and Attendance

Record absences, overtime, and planned working time, then evaluate them for payroll readiness.

05

PC00

Payroll Processing

Run payroll by area and period, compute deductions, and post results to Finance and Controlling.

06

PHAP_CREATE

Performance Appraisals

Create appraisal documents, capture goals and KPI ratings, and support compensation decisions.

07

PA40 Leaving

Separation

Trigger the leaving action, stop payroll participation, end-date assignments, and archive records.

Highlights

Unique Points

  • True end-to-end integration across the entire lifecycle.
  • Infotype-based historical architecture with full auditability.
  • Date-effective change management for future-dated HR updates.
  • Automatic payroll-to-finance posting.
  • Support for India-specific compliance like PF, ESI, PT, and TDS.

Conclusion

Why This Matters

The H2R process in SAP HR gives organizations a unified source of truth for workforce data while keeping payroll, time, appraisal, and organizational structures connected. The result is better compliance, cleaner reporting, and far less manual effort across the employee lifecycle.
